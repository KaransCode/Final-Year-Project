About Camlen

Camlen University is a college founded in 1877 in Providence, RI. Today 2,538 students engage in 44 full-time bachelor’s and master’s degree programs, supported by a committed faculty and worldwide network of more than 32,000 alumni.

By cultivating expansive and elastic thinking, CU(Camlen University ) equips artists, designers and scholars to generate and challenge the ideas that shape our world.

Our History

Started by a group of women well before any woman had the right to vote in the US, Camlen has always embraced art and design’s capacity to stoke curiosity and spur progress.

Strategic plan

How can Camlen unique approach to learning, thinking and making evolve in the 21st century? Our strategic plan outlines the ways our current creative practices respond to the most critical social, political and environmental challenges we face today.

Leadership

Guided by President Crystal Williams, our leadership furthers the reach and champions the transformational power of creative education.
